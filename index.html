Who We Are

Building a Better India, One Life at a Time

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). We are a Central Government-licensed, Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

Holding 80G & 12A certifications, we ensure every rupee donated is tax-exempt and fully accounted for. We are also CSR-1 and NITI Aayog registered, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified — reflecting our commitment to transparent, high-quality operations.

Our Causes

Six Projects, One Mission

Each initiative targets a distinct vulnerability in our communities — together they form an interconnected approach to sustainable social change.

Education

Project BachpanShala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children — nurturing young minds and building brighter futures across India.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence — soaring towards a brighter, self-reliant future.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Rescue, protection, and feeding of stray and injured animals — because compassion extends to every living being.

Community Service

Project Sewa

Providing food and clothing to the underprivileged — serving humanity with compassion, one meal and one smile at a time.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation and sustainability efforts — planting trees and protecting ecosystems for a better tomorrow.

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through practical skill development programs, bridging the gap between potential and opportunity.

Social Impact

Why Our Work Matters
